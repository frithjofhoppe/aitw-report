4.1 Overview of Provider Concepts

4.2 Similarities Across Providers
•	Landing zones as foundational cloud environments 
•	Shared emphasis on controlled and structured cloud adoption 
•	Recurrent focus on security, governance, and consistency 
4.3 Differences Across Providers
•	Differences in terminology and conceptual framing 
•	Differences in structural organization 
•	Differences in the treatment of security, governance, and scalability 
•	Differences in how strongly the concept is tied to provider-specific implementations

4.4.5 Standardization and Automation
•	Comparison of how providers support repeatable, consistent, and automated setup
